Job Description

We are looking for an experienced Senior Frontend Developer to join our engineering team. You will be responsible for building and maintaining high-quality web applications, collaborating with designers and backend engineers, and mentoring junior developers.


Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain front-end applications using React, TypeScript
  • Collaborate with UI/UX designers to implement pixel-perfect designs
  • Write clean, testable, and well-documented code
  • Mentor and guide junior team members
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to best practices

Requirements:

  • 5+ years of experience in frontend development
  • Strong proficiency in JavaScript, TypeScript, React
  • Experience with state management solutions (Redux, Zustand)
  • Familiarity with REST APIs and GraphQL
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills
